## 2. Product Summary

MemorySong is a family-oriented custom song platform where customers can turn personal stories, memories, and emotional messages into beautiful custom songs for loved ones.

Customers place an order by sharing details about the recipient, occasion, relationship, memories, preferred artist, genre, mood, and delivery deadline. The platform then uses a hybrid workflow of AI-assisted songwriting, AI/music-generation tools, human review, and optional real artist/band production to create and deliver a personalized song.

The final song is delivered via email and a private share page where the customer can listen, download, view lyrics, and request revisions.

## 3. Core Promise

> Turn your story into a song they will never forget.

The platform is not positioned as a generic AI music tool. It is positioned as an emotional gift service powered by a family band, artists, storytellers, and production technology.

A customer creates a song for their partner for an anniversary, proposal, birthday, Valentine’s Day, or long-distance relationship.

### Family Tribute

A customer creates a song for their mother, father, grandparent, sibling, child, or family member.

A customer creates a song for a best friend using shared jokes, memories, and emotional moments.

A customer creates a custom song for a couple, wedding entrance, first dance, or wedding gift.

A customer creates a sincere apology song for someone they hurt or miss.

A customer creates a respectful tribute for someone they lost or want to honor.

## 6. Product Goals

### Business Goals

- Sell personalized custom songs as emotional digital gifts.
- Build a recognizable family-oriented music gifting brand.
- Use social media storytelling to drive organic and paid traffic.
- Create repeat purchases around birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, Valentine’s Day, and holidays.

15. AI Songwriting Pipeline
Input

The customer’s form data.

Output

Structured song brief, lyric outline, final lyrics, music prompt.

Step 1: Normalize Order Data

Convert all form fields into one internal JSON object.

Example:

{
  "recipient_name": "Maya",
  "recipient_nickname": "May",
  "mention_name_preference": "nickname",
  "relationship": "wife",
  "occasion": "anniversary",
  "genre": "soft R&B ballad",
  "mood": "romantic, nostalgic, warm",
  "story": "We met in Taiwan at a coffee shop...",
  "favorite_memories": "Rainy walks, mango smoothies...",
  "inside_jokes": "She calls me stubborn...",
  "avoid": "Do not make it too sad",
  "desired_feeling": ["loved", "seen", "appreciated"]
}

The AI turns the messy story into a structured creative brief.

Output:

{
  "song_title_ideas": ["Mango Rain", "Five Years of Us"],

  "must_avoid": [

    "Verse 1",

    "Chorus",
    "Verse 2",
    "Bridge",
    "Final Chorus"
  ]

16. Music Generation / Production Pipeline

The platform should support three production modes.

Mode A: AI-Generated Song

Used for lower-tier packages.

Workflow:

Approved lyrics
→ Music generation API
→ Draft MP3 created
→ Admin review
→ Final delivery
Mode B: AI-Assisted + Human-Polished Song

Recommended default model.

Workflow:

AI creates lyrics
→ Human editor improves lyrics
→ AI creates song draft
→ Human reviews
→ Optional vocal/arrangement improvement
→ Final master delivered
Mode C: Fully Human Band Song

Used for premium package.

Workflow:

Human songwriter writes lyrics
→ Band/producer composes melody
→ Artist records vocals
→ Producer mixes/master
→ Final files delivered
